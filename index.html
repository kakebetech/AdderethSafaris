About Us

We Provide Best Tour Packages In Your Budget

With over 7 years of dynamic engagement in the tourism industry, Mrs. Adong Margaret N has established herself as a preeminent expert in curating extraordinary travel experiences. Driven by an unwavering passion for showcasing Uganda’s splendor, she founded Addereth Safaris, a pioneering tour operator illuminating the nation’s hidden gems for the global community. Expertise: Mrs. Adong Margaret N possesses unparalleled expertise in Uganda’s tourism landscape, honed through collaborative partnerships with diverse travel entities and leading numerous expeditions across the country. Her areas of proficiency include: - Immersive cultural journeys - Thrilling adventure expeditions - Enchanting wildlife safaris - Community-centric tourism initiatives Company Overview: Addereth Safaris is a leading tour operator in Uganda’s tourism industry, dedicated to crafting authentic and enriching experiences for travelers. We champion sustainable tourism principles, supporting local communities and conservation efforts to ensure mutually beneficial outcomes for both the local economy and ecology.
