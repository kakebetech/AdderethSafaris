Vision

To be the leading tour operator in Uganda, renowned for our dedication to excellence, sustainability, and community empowerment. We aspire to showcase the magnificence of Uganda while promoting responsible tourism that benefits both our travelers and the local communities we engage with. Our vision is to create unforgettable memories for our clients, fostering a deep appreciation for Uganda’s rich cultural heritage and natural wonders.

Mission

Our mission at Addereth Safaris is to deliver authentic and enriching travel experiences that reveal the hidden gems of Uganda. We are committed to:

  • Crafting personalized journeys that highlight the cultural, natural, and adventurous aspects of Uganda.

  • Supporting sustainable tourism practices that protect and preserve the environment.

  • Empowering local communities through tourism initiatives that promote socio-economic development.

  • Providing exceptional customer service that ensures every traveler’s needs and expectations are met.

Core Values

Excellence

We strive for the highest standards in everything we do, from planning and execution to customer service and experience delivery.

Sustainability

We are dedicated to promoting eco-friendly tourism practices that minimize environmental impact and contribute to the preservation of Uganda’s natural beauty.

Community Empowerment

We believe in giving back to the communities we visit. Our tours and initiatives are designed to support local economies and improve the quality of life for local residents.

Authenticity

We provide genuine and immersive experiences that allow our travelers to connect deeply with Uganda’s culture, nature, and people.

Integrity

We operate with honesty, transparency, and respect, building trust with our clients, partners, and communities.

Innovation

We continually seek innovative ways to enhance our services, ensuring our travelers enjoy unique and memorable experiences.

Customer-Centric Approach

Our clients are at the heart of everything we do. We are committed to understanding and exceeding their expectations, delivering personalized and exceptional service.

By living these core values, we aim to create a positive impact on both our travelers and the communities we touch, ensuring a sustainable and enriching tourism experience for all.
